About Us

We're dedicated to ensuring you have a seriously fantastic time and amazingly good value experience

Bowling is one of the original social networks, it's been a part of our families and friendships for generations, from birthday parties to first dates. With approximately 1000 lanes, bowling is at the beating heart of what we do.

Wait your turn to bowl in comfort on our leather sofas enjoying the atmosphere. All lanes are fully accessible to wheelchair users and are fitted with side bumpers to ensure the best chance of a STRIKE every time!!
